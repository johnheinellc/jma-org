Our story

JMA is a values-driven movement built for people and purpose.

JMA exists as a values-driven movement centered on people and purpose. This is a window of opportunity to build something that lasts—because this is a calling, not just an organization. We don't take over—we create the structure, support, and space others need to move forward.

We choose formation over fast results, stewardship over shortcuts, and partnership over control. As a collaborative ecosystem of people and organizations living on mission, we move together—turning vision into action and strengthening what's already been placed within, not replacing it.

We're passionate about shining a light on the talent, craft, and heart behind every musician and author we work with. Through promotional concerts, book readings, podcasts, and collaborative performances, we create meaningful opportunities to showcase your work, share your story, and connect with audiences who resonate with your passion.

Whether you're launching a new album or highlighting one of your favorite titles, we're here to help your voice be heard and your creativity celebrated.

Our mission

We exist to support resilient, mission-driven work that can flourish long after the first launch.
